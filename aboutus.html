About us

We are a small local volleyball club called Slammers Beach Volleyball. At Slammers we have room for all kind of players - every age both beginners and competitive. There are about 100 people playing at Slammers each week.


Beyond volleyball, we also have a lot of social events and a licensed bar with happy hour every day from 6-7pm, available for hire for functions.   

Our teams are doing a great job. Check out the ladder for the results. Sign up and get your teams name on it.
